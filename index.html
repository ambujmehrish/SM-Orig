AudioPaLM

A Large Language Model That Can Speak and Listen

| paper |

Paul Rubenstein*, Chulayuth Asawaroengchai*, Duc Dung Nguyen*, … Neil Zeghidour, Yu Zhang, Zhishuai Zhang, Lukas Zilka, Christian Frank

Google

Abstract. We introduce AudioPaLM, a large language model for speech understanding and generation. AudioPaLM fuses PaLM-2 [Anil et al., 2023] and AudioLM [Borsos et al., 2022] into a unified multimodal architecture that can process and generate text and speech with applications including speech recognition and speech-to-speech translation. …

AudioPaLM Overview

We expand the embedding matrix of a pretrained text-only model to handle audio tokens. A mixed sequence of text and audio tokens is fed as input; the model decodes text or audio, which latter AudioLM stages convert back to raw audio.
